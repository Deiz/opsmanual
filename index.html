OKF Operations Manual¶

Welcome to the OKF Operations Manual. This is the canonical source for information on the operations and infrastructure of the Open Knowledge Foundation.

Note

The Operations Manual is new, and content is still being migrated from its various previous homes (Google Drive, the OKFN wiki, peoples’ heads). Please bear with us while we get the manual up to scratch.
